Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 27, 2025

Thank you for using Zeus Bot. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our Discord bot and website. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring your data is handled responsibly.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Zeus Bot, we collect and process the following types of information:

  • Discord User Information: User ID, username, discriminator, avatar, and roles within servers where Zeus Bot is present.
  • Server Information: Server IDs, channel IDs, role IDs, and server settings where Zeus Bot is used.
  • Configuration Data: Custom settings configured for the bot within your server.
  • Usage Data: Command usage, interactions with the bot, and error logs to improve performance.
  • Authentication Information: When you log in to our dashboard, we receive authentication information from Discord's OAuth2 system.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • To provide, maintain, and improve the bot's functionality
  • To process and execute commands requested by users
  • To customize the bot's features based on server settings
  • To authenticate users on our dashboard
  • To ensure the security of our services
  • To troubleshoot technical issues and fix bugs
  • To analyze usage patterns and optimize user experience

3. Data Storage and Security

We take data security seriously and implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information:

  • All data is stored on secure servers with restricted access
  • We employ encryption for sensitive information
  • Regular security audits and updates are performed
  • Access to data is limited to authorized personnel only

4. Data Retention and Deletion

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide our services. If you remove Zeus Bot from your server, your server-specific configuration data will be deleted automatically after 30 days.

You can request deletion of your personal data by contacting us through our support server or email. We will process such requests within 30 days.

5. Third-Party Services

Zeus Bot may integrate with third-party services to enhance functionality. These services have their own privacy policies and terms. While using our bot, you might also be subject to the terms and privacy policies of:

  • Discord (https://discord.com/privacy)
  • YouTube and other music streaming platforms (for music functionality)
  • Other third-party APIs used to provide certain features

6. Children's Privacy

Zeus Bot is not directed to children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will promptly delete such information.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this Privacy Policy. We encourage users to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.
